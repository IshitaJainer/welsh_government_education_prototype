Attainment information

The Welsh Government is developing a new approach to reporting on school outcomes as part of a reformed information ecosystem. From Summer 2027, reporting will be organised around the 14 to 16 Learner Entitlement Indicators Framework (LEIF), which covers the four components of the 14 to 16 learner entitlement:

  • Qualifications related to literacy and numeracy
  • Qualifications to encourage breadth
  • Wider learning and experiences across the curriculum
  • Reflections on learning and progress and post-16 planning
