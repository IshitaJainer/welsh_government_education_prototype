What do these terms mean?
Capped 9 points score
A combined score across a learner's best 9 qualifications, capped to prevent any single subject dominating. The interim measures version applies while the new curriculum is being phased in.
Literacy points score
The average points score achieved in literacy-related qualifications, including English or Welsh language and literature.
Numeracy points score
The average points score achieved in mathematics and numeracy qualifications.
Science points score
The average points score achieved in science qualifications, including GCSE sciences and VCSE alternatives.
Welsh Baccalaureate Skills Challenge Certificate points score
The average points score for the Skills Challenge Certificate, a core part of the Welsh Baccalaureate assessing skills such as critical thinking, enterprise and communication.
Free school meals (FSM) — 3 year average
The percentage of learners eligible for free school meals, averaged over three years to reduce year-on-year variation. Used as an indicator of socioeconomic disadvantage.
Pupil Teacher Ratio (PTR)
The number of learners per teacher. A higher ratio means more learners per teacher. This does not account for support staff or part-time arrangements.
School budget per pupil
The total school budget divided by the number of learners. Includes funding from the local authority and Welsh Government grants, but excludes some capital or project funding.
